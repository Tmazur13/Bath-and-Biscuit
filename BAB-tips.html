Pet Grooming Tips

Follow these tips to keep your pet looking their best and staying healthy between grooming sessions:

  • Regular Brushing: Brush your pet's coat regularly to prevent mats and tangles, this is especially helpful for long-haired breeds.
  • Ear Cleaning: Clean your pet's ears regularly to prevent infections, especially if they have floppy ears.
  • Nail Trimming: Keep your pet's nails trimmed to prevent discomfort and injury. Visit us for a quick trim.
